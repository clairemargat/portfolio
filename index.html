Product Designer at We Share Trust

I collaborated with the Product Manager to design the MVP of a Software as a Service from the ground up

We Share Trust is a French start-up that makes e-commerce leaders products available to a panel of voluntary consumers who have chosen to comply with the requirements of an independent test.

Testers receive the product at home and must use it for a minimum period before writing their review. Each product test is conducted at the testerâ€™s home and remotely supervised by a test expert.

All reviews are checked to ensure the accuracy and sincerity of the opinions expressed. The WeShareTrust teams also verify that the product has been properly used, notably through a phone interview and a detailed analysis of each review prior to moderation.

The client contacted me to help conceiving a moderation reviews software capable to effectively answer business and users needs.
